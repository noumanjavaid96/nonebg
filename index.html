01 — The thesis

Talent is everywhere.
Access is not.

The next generation of great companies is being built in Karachi and Dubai, in Riyadh, Jakarta, and Singapore. What those builders lack is not ambition or ability — it's a bridge: distribution in Western markets, capital that understands both sides, and operators who have made the crossing before.

East Bridge Global exists to be that bridge. One ecosystem — ventures, academy, alliance, and growth — built to move founders, agencies, and capital across borders with confidence, and to turn regional momentum into global market share.
